Data Mobility Exploitation

While such solutions succedded at providing users with interesting services, many obstacles are still to be addressed in order to protect users' data. The aim of this PoC is therefore to evaluate the usage of BlockChain technologies to efficiently exploit users' mobility data.

Decentralized Energy Market Place

Many proofs of concept have been presented since 2016 around the use of blockchain in the energy sector. This phenomenon is favored by the growth of decentralized renewable energies, combined with the massive introduction of information and communication technologies. As a technology for storing and transmitting data in a decentralized manner, the Blockchain has been seen as a new way to facilitate the creation of local green energy exchange networks. In this context, we aim in this PoC at evaluating the use of blockchain technology to create a Decentralized Energy Marketplace. Besides, we build a simulation and optimization platform to evaluate the technical feasibility of the proposed solution.

Multicriteria Route Planning

The human mobility is nowadays always organized in a multimodal context. However, the transport system has become more complex. Consequently, for the sake of helping passengers, building Advanced Travelers Information Systems (ATIS) has become a certain need. Since passengers tend to consider several other criteria than the travel time, an efﬁcient routing system should incorporate a multi-objective analysis. The aim of this project is to build a Framework whereby passengers are provided with alternative routes the meet their travel criteria.

Stochastic Route Planning

Modern transport systems are nowadays very complex. Building Advanced Travelers Information Systems (ATIS) has therefore become a certain need. Since passengers do not only seek a short-time travel, but they tend to optimize several criteria, an efficient routing system should incorporate a multi- objective analysis into its search process. Besides, the transport system may behave in an uncertain manner. Integrating uncertainty into routing algorithms may thus provide more robust itineraries. Therefore the aim of this project is to build a platform tat provides passengers with robust itineraries while accomplishing their travels.

ANGRY UTBM

the aim of the project was to develop a game similar to angry bird. In the Angry UTBM, players use a slingshot to launch birds at people(ennemies) stationed on or within various structures, with the intent of destroying all the people on the playing field. As players pass levels, new types of birds become available. Each type of birds is characterized by the number and the category of the eggs that it can launch. Ennemies were designed in the form of moved object; each types of them has its own speed and design.

Handover Simulation

In this project, I deveeloped a similator to mimick the handover process in cellular telecommunications. The terms handover refers to the process of transferring an ongoing call or data session from one channel connected to the core network to another channel. In satellite communications it is the process of transferring satellite control responsibility from one earth station to another without loss or interruption of service.
